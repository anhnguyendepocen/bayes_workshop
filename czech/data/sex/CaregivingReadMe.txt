### Data ###
Columns:
(1) Testes Size: Testes volume (i.e., residuals after regressing volume against height).
(2) Caregiving: Level of paternal caregiving as determined by parenting questionnaires.

Rows: Each row corresponds to one father.

### Source ###
Mascaro, J. S., Hackett, P. D., & Rilling, J. K. (2013). Testicular volume is inversely correlated 
with nurturing-related brain activity in human fathers. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, 
110, 15746-15751. Data obtained from the original article using PlotDigitizer.
